# The School Website

## Guide to Publishing News on WordPress with Italia Design Template

**Premise:** This guide is designed for those who are already somewhat familiar with the WordPress interface. If you are a beginner, I recommend also consulting the official documentation of the Italia Design theme.

### **1. Access the WordPress Dashboard**
* Open your browser and type your WordPress site address followed by /wp-admin.
* Enter username and password and click "Login".

### **2. Create a New Article**
* In the left menu, under "Posts", click on "Add New".
* The WordPress editor will open.

### **3. Write Your Article**
* **Title:** Enter the title of your news in the appropriate box.
* **Content:** Write the text of the news directly in the visual editor or the block editor, depending on your preferences.
* **Featured Image:**
  * Click on "Set featured image".
  * Select the image you want to use as a preview for the news.
  * You can crop it and choose the most suitable size.

### **4. Format the Text**
* **Using blocks:** The Italia Design theme is often configured to use the WordPress block editor. This allows you to easily insert images, videos, quotes, and other multimedia elements.
* **Styles:** The Italia Design theme offers a set of predefined styles for headings, paragraphs, and other text elements. Use these styles to maintain consistent formatting with the rest of the site.

### **5. Categories and Tags**
* **Categories:** Assign your news to the most appropriate category. Categories serve to organize your content and make it easier to search.
* **Tags:** Add tags to describe the contents of your news with specific keywords. Tags can help you improve your site's SEO.

### **6. Publication Settings**
* **Publish date:** Choose the date and time you want your news to be published.
* **Visibility:** Choose whether the news should be public, private, or password protected.
* **Publish:** Once you are satisfied with your article, click the "Publish" button.

### **7. SEO Optimization**
* **SEO Title:** Make sure the title of your news is clear, concise, and contains the most important keywords.
* **Meta description:** Write a brief description of your news that entices users to click.
* **Images:** Use high-quality images optimized for the web.
* **Internal links:** Link your news to other articles on your site to improve user navigation.

### **8. Preview**
* Before publishing, click the "Preview" button to see how your news will look on the site.

### **Additional Tips:**
* **Use shortcodes:** The Italia Design theme might offer specific shortcodes to insert custom elements, such as info boxes or buttons.
* **Consult theme documentation:** The Italia Design theme documentation will provide detailed information on its features and customization options.
* **Ask for help:** If you have difficulties, you can ask the WordPress community or your web developer for help.

**Observations:**
* **Customizations:** The exact look of the editor and customization options may vary slightly depending on the version of the Italia Design theme and any modifications made by your developer.
* **Plugins:** If you have installed additional plugins, they might offer extra features for content management.

**By following these steps, you will be able to publish high-quality news on your school WordPress site and keep your community updated.**
