## Guide to First Access and Use of Argo DidUp for Parents

**What you need:**

* **Smartphone or tablet:** With compatible operating system (iOS or Android).
* **Login credentials:** Provided by the school (school code, user code, and password).

**Procedure:**

1. **Download the app:**
   * Open the App Store (for iOS devices) or Google Play Store (for Android devices).
   * Search for "Argo DidUp" and download the official app.

2. **Install the app:**
   * Follow the on-screen instructions to complete the installation.

3. **Launch the app:**
   * Once installed, open the Argo DidUp app.

4. **First login:**
   * Enter the **credentials** provided by the school:
     * **School code:** Alphanumeric code that identifies your school.
     * **User code:** Usually the tax code or an identification code assigned by the school.
     * **Password:** The password that was provided to you.
   * **Login:** Click on the "Login" or "Enter" button.

5. **Change password (optional):**
   * Upon first login, you will be asked to **change the password** for security reasons. Choose a strong password and memorize it.

6. **Explore the app:**
   * The Argo DidUp interface may vary slightly depending on the version and school configuration, but generally, you will find the following main sections:
     * **Notice Board:** Here you will find important communications from the school, teachers, and general news.
     * **Register:** View your child's absences, grades, and behaviors.
     * **Homework:** You will find homework assigned by professors, deadlines, and the ability to attach completed work.
     * **Communications:** Here you can send private messages to teachers or school staff.

**Daily use:**

* **Check regularly:** Check the app regularly to stay updated on school communications and your child's academic progress.
* **Communications with teachers:** Use the messaging function to communicate with teachers regarding doubts, absences, or other aspects of your child's school life.
* **Homework:** Check assigned homework, deadlines, and help your child organize to complete them.
* **Register:** Keep an eye on the register to monitor your child's attendance and academic performance.

**Useful tips:**

* **Notifications:** Enable push notifications to receive important alerts in real-time.
* **Updates:** Make sure you always have the latest version of the app installed to take advantage of new features and improvements.
* **Assistance:** In case of difficulty, consult the app guide or contact the school secretariat.

**Important notes:**
* **Privacy:** The information contained in the app is confidential and protected.
* **School configuration:** The features and appearance of the app may vary slightly depending on the school configuration.

**With this guide, you should be able to easily use Argo DidUp and stay up to date on your child's school life.**

**Need further assistance?**
* **Contact the school:** The school secretariat is at your disposal for any clarification or technical problem.
* **Argo Support:** Consult the Argo Software website to find information and assistance.

**I hope this guide is useful to you!**

**Note:** For a more specific guide, I recommend consulting the user manual provided by your school or searching for specific online tutorials for the version of Argo DidUp used by your school.
